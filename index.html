CompClub

What is Computer Club? It is a place where you can use our powerful computers for any purpose you need! You can have fun with your friends by playing computer games or do your job here with the most suitable computers chosen for you.

Why us?

So you can give a question. "Why we should choose you?". The answer is simple. Because we provide you the best service and devices to use. One of our main advantages is comfort. Our gaming stations are equipped with high-quality monitors, comfortable chairs, and fast internet connection. This allows you to enjoy long sessions without feeling tired. We also regularly update our equipment to make sure you always get the best performance.


At CompClub, we care about our community. Our friendly staff is always ready to help you with any technical issues or questions. We maintain a clean and safe atmosphere where everyone feels welcome, from beginners to experienced gamers.


In addition, CompClub is a great place to spend time with friends. You can play together, compete in tournaments, or simply relax and have fun. Reasonable prices and flexible time options make our club accessible to everyone.

Which computers do we use

In our club there are two zones depending on for what you came. We have Work Zone and Game Zone and each zone has their own computers.

Work Zone

  • Intel Core i7 / Ryzen 7
  • RTX 3060 / RTX 4070
  • 32–64 GB RAM
  • Dual monitors

Ideal for programming, video editing, design, and 3D work.

Game Zone

  • Intel Core i7 / Ryzen 7
  • RTX 3060–3070
  • 32 GB RAM
  • 144–165 Hz monitors

Perfect for competitive gaming and AAA titles.
